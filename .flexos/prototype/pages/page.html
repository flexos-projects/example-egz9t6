Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our process and partnership.

What types of companies do you work with?
We partner with a diverse range of organizations, from ambitious startups to established DAX-listed enterprises, across various industries including technology, manufacturing, and finance. Our focus is on clients who are committed to strategic change and sustainable growth.
What is your typical engagement process?
Our process begins with a deep-dive discovery phase to understand your unique context. This is followed by collaborative strategy design, where we develop a clear roadmap. We then partner with you through the implementation phase and conclude with a performance review to ensure targets are met and value is delivered.
How do you measure success?
Success is defined collaboratively at the outset of every engagement. We establish clear, measurable Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) that align with your strategic business objectives. Our success is measured by our ability to help you achieve these targets and deliver tangible, long-term value.
